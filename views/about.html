About Us

The Event Seating Planner is a user-friendly tool designed to simplify the process of arranging seating for various events, such as convocation, conferences, and parties.

Features

  • Interactive Layout: Easily create and modify seating arrangements.
  • Guest Management: Organize guest lists and book seats efficiently.
  • Collaboration: Work together with others to finalize arrangements.
  • Visualizations: View seating plans in a clear, visual format.

Mission

Our mission is to enhance the event planning experience by providing a straightforward solution for seating arrangements, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience for all attendees.
